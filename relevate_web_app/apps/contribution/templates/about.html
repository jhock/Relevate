About Us {% if can_edit %} Add New Entry {% endif %}

Relevate is an interdisciplinary team of relationship scientists, educators, and practitioners in partnership with computer engineers, marketing specialists, and young adults who are passionate about improving the health of individuals and relationships everywhere. Although social scientists have been researching what makes relationships thrive for decades, there is a huge gap between what researchers know about healthy relationships and what is accessible to the general public.

Our aim is to fuse technology and scientific discovery to elevate the lives of people across the world on their journey in and between relationships through MyRelevate, a mobile platform for relationship scholars and practitioners to directly share their research and theory-driven insights with people seeking resources for making their relationship goals a reality.
