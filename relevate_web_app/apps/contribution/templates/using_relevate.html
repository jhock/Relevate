  • Getting Started

    The following guide will instruct you on how to navigate the website, create a user account, create an application to be a contributor to the site, as well as creation and editing of posts.

    Set up user account

    You must set up a basic user account before creating a contributor account. To do so, click on "CREATE ACCOUNT" in the navigation bar, or the link here. Fill out the e-mail and password forms, and the optional topic preferences. These preferences will be used to select relevant posts to display on the home page. If you do not select any topic, your home page posts will be filled out at random.

    Confirm Your Account

    Confirm your account by opening the inbox of the e-mail address used to create your account. Open the confirmation email sent from Relevate, and click the link included. Your user account will them be confirmed.

    Apply to be a contributor

    Once you have set up a user account, you have access to the contributor application. To access it, click on "CONTRIBUTOR APPLICATION" on the navigation bar, or the link here . Before you begin, it is important to note that the information entered into the form will not be saved if the browser or window is closed, and it it best to complete the form in one sitting. There are four sections to be completed before the application will be accepted. All required entries will be marked with an orange asterisk. For the first section, enter in your degree and click the "ADD" button. If you wish to remove an entry, simply click the "DELETE" button and re-enter the information. Once you have completed a section, click on the next section to expand it. Click on a topic in the "List of Available Topics" to add it to your application, and click on a topic in the "Selected Topics for Expertise" to remove it. Hover over a topic for a short description. Next fill out the "Professional Information" section, and read over then agree to the terms and conditions. Finally, click the blue "Submit" button once you are satisfied to submit the application.

    Create a new Relevate post.

    To create a new post, click the "POSTS" button in the navigation bar, followed by the "Create A New Post" link that appears underneath. This link will bring you to a page with three options of "Article", "Link", and "Infographic". Each option will have entries that are suitable for it's own content. Click on the appropriate blue box, and fill out the form. Note that all entries on the form are required in order to create the post; none are optional. An image is always required in order to maintain consistency between posts, and to make them more appealing to readers. Finally, to submit and publish the post at the same time, click the orange "SAVE AND PUBLISH" button. Your post will automatically appear on the home page. If you are not yet ready to publish the post, click the blue "SAVE" button and your post will be saved for editing and publishing at a later time.

    IMPORTANT NOTE: If you navigate to another page or close the browser, your post will not be saved. You must click the "SAVE" or "SAVE AND PUBLISH" buttons to preserve it.

    Edit, manage, and delete posts

    Your posts will be found by clicking the "POSTS" button in the navigation bar, then the "Posts To Date" link that appears underneath. This page allows you to view all the posts you have ever created including both published and saved posts, only your published posts, and only your saved posts. Saved posts are posts that you have created, but have not yet published. Finish and publish a saved post by clicking on the blue "EDIT" button of the post under "SAVED POSTS", finishing the post, and clicking the orange "SAVE AND PUBLISH" button. Delete a post by clicking the red "DELETE" button next to the post you wish to delete.

    Join the Public Scholarship and Content Creation group.

    Joining the Public Scholarship and Content Creation group is by invitation or request only. Contact Amber Vennum or another member of the Relevate group to request to join. You will be notified when you are granted privileges to create content for both the Public Scholarship and Content Creation sections.

  • Troubleshooting and F.A.Q.

    How can I stay updated on Relevate events?

    Keep up to date with relevate events on our Facebook page. (link)

    I cannot access the site from my iPad, tablet, phone, etc.

    The relevate site is not developed for mobile users or contributors. Contributors must use a full-sized laptop or desktop to create, update, or manage their content, while users may download the mobile app for browsing articles. (app is under development)

    I lost my user account confirmation e-mail.

    If you have lost your confirmation e-mail, request a new one to be sent by clicking the "Confirm Account" button on the navigation bar, or the following link here.

    I have forgotten my password. How can I log in?

    To reset your password, click the "Forgot your password?" link located on the bottom of the login page, or the following link here. A reset link will be sent to the e-mail address associated with your account, which will allow you to create a new password. You will not be able to recover your old password.

    Is my personal information secure on this site?

    Yes, your information is stored only on our secure server, and is not shared with any other entities. Your information is only used to better provide posts, articles, and other content that is suited to you.

    Where can I add a suggestion for the site or report a problem?

    Send an e-mail to myrelevate@outlook.com with your suggestion or problem. Relevate is a community driven site, and benefits from the feedback of users and contributors.

    How can I find a specific post or find a post on a specific topic?

    To search for a post, enter in a section of the post's title or the topic you are searching for and click enter on your keyboard. Any posts matching your search will be displayed. Note that currently Relevate Resources Posts are not searchable.
